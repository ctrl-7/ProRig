About-Us

We build gaming systems because we love gaming.

It’s at the core of every computer we build, every decision we come to, and every tweet we tweet. From day one onward, we have been committed to maintaining that passion, and putting it into each of the gaming systems we create. Our philosophy is that anyone can do something well, but passion is what truly inspires excellence. We take pride in the meticulousness our team demonstrates on a daily basis, and that pride makes it easy for us to firmly stand behind our products. That’s why we offer one of the best warranties, and have one of the best in-house customer support teams in the industry.

The Process

If it isn’t perfect, it doesn’t leave our facility.

1. Gather

All of your custom components are gathered in our warehouse and put in queue.

2. Assemble

Your PC is meticulously assembled by an Ironside technician, then reviewed by a senior technician before being moved into testing.

3. Test

Windows is installed on your system, followed by multiple stress and benchmark programs that are designed to heavily stress each individual component in your computer.

4. Ship

Cushioned by custom internal foam, your system is double boxed for extra security and sent safely on its way to you.
